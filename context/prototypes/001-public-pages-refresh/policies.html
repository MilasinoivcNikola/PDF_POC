How your book is made

Every book is illustrated with the help of AI image generation, guided from a photo of your own pet, and then reviewed by a person before it's sent to you. We believe in being honest about this: the art is made with a machine, finished by a human, and made for you. The exact wording of our AI-honesty statement is still being decided.

Refunds & remakes

We make each book to order, from your own photos and words, and every one is looked over by a person before we send it — so it should feel right when it reaches you. If something still isn't — a detail we got wrong, or an illustration that just doesn't feel like your pet — tell us within 30 days of delivery and we'll remake it, at no cost.

If we try and still can't make it something you're glad to keep, we'll refund you in full. You don't have to explain or justify; grief is hard enough.

Your privacy

Your photo and the details you share are used only to make your book. Our full privacy policy is being written and will appear here before ordering opens.
