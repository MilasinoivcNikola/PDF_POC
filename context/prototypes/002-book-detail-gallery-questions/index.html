The questions you’ll answer

Set aside 5–10 minutes and have one good photo ready. Here’s everything we’ll ask — gather your answers first, or simply read the example we used to paint the sample above.

  • Illustration styleRequiredWatercolor · Storybook · Pencil

The child

  • The child’s nameRequired
  • Their ageRequired3–5 · 6–8 · 9–12 — this sets the reading level and tone.
03

The memories

  • Their favorite thing to doRequired
  • Where they loved to sleepRequired
  • A favorite memoryRequiredOne small moment. The longer pages of the book grow out of this.
  • A dedicationOptionalA line for the first page, if you’d like one.
04

Tone & gentle choices

  • How they diedOptionalOld age · illness · suddenly · with a doctor’s help. Shapes the goodbye gently — leave blank to keep it soft.
  • Where the book says they are nowOptionalRainbow Bridge · heaven · kept in our hearts · no afterlife — your family’s words.
  • Other pets at homeOptionalSo the story can include the ones still waiting at home.
