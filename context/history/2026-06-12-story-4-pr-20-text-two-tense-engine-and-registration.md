- **The two-tense engine (the headline + the #1 risk).** A `[LIVING_OR_MEMORIAL]` toggle (default **living** = present tense, celebration / **memorial** = past tense, grief). `lib/story/story4/variants.ts` `composeVariants4()` builds **each page body whole via per-tense builders** (living vs memorial side-by-side functions taking the tense as an argument) rather than "swap-then-patch" — so a present-tense sentence structurally cannot leak into a memorial letter or vice versa. It then layers relationship (couple → "you both" / "my favorites"), species voice (pages 2/3/4), and the gift-for cover inscription; **memorial only** appends the Page-5 death-type seam line + belief-frame closing. Sparse/blank `quirks` → stock Page-3 fallback (both tenses); `species: "other"` → species-neutral voice. `resolveStory4(s) = mergeStory4(composeVariants4(s), s)`.
