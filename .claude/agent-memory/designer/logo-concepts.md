Iteration `003-logo-concepts` proposed replacing the plain-text header wordmark
(`BRAND` string + the 5-blob paw `.wordmark__ornament` in `SiteHeader.tsx` /
`SiteFooter.tsx`) with a real logo. Five directions, each shown as hero lockup +
live nav + light/gold/rose surfaces + 48/32/16px favicon.

**Why:** PM wants a real mark, not text. Must hold both emotional worlds (gold =
living/celebration, rose = loss/memorial) and stay on the existing design system.

**How to apply:** when the logo conversation resumes, start from these five rather
than re-exploring from scratch. **DECIDED (iteration 004): the PM chose Direction 03
"Open Heart-Book"** (open book whose pages form a heart) — NOT my 003 recommendation
of 02. Treat 03 as the locked glyph baseline. Also decided in 004: (a) two-worlds
tint — glyph neutral ink in the global header, gold on living/celebration pages, rose
on loss/memorial pages (only strokes+spine take the tint; the rose-faint page-wash
stays); (b) favicon = the full heart-book at favicon scale, transparent bg, a
size-tuned redraw (NOT a bare heart or monogram). **Still open:** the "Dearbound"
wordmark treatment — I recommended D, letter-spaced small-caps (over title-case
control / lowercase / tracked caps), awaiting PM pick. See `004-heart-book-lockup/`.

The directions:
- **01 Refined Paw** — system today cleaned up; lowest risk, leans "pet brand."
- **02 The Bound Spine** *(recommended)* — sewn book-spine glyph + gold stitch; names the product, puns "-bound", neutral across both worlds, best-degrading non-monogram favicon.
- **03 Open Heart-Book** — book-pages-as-heart; most sentimental, loses the book at favicon size.
- **04 Tucked Paw** *(runner-up)* — dog-eared page + small paw; pet + book in one form.
- **05 Monogram** — Fraunces "D"-as-spined-book in a colophon seal; most press-like, best favicon, least warm/least "pet."

Open questions left for the PM: (1) which direction; (2) glyph color fixed sitewide
vs. tinted per-world (my lean: fixed); (3) favicon colored-chip vs. transparent;
(4) leave the "Dearbound" wordmark casing/spacing as-is. See the folder's NOTES.md.
